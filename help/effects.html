Effects

To check which effects you currently have on type /check_effects_online. If you wish to only see a shorter list of effects you can set that list with the /effects_list comand. The effects need to be standardized by replacing spaces with underscores and removing any non-alphanumeric characters. Once you have your /effects_list set you can use the /check_effects (shows a complete list of all effects, online and offline) and /check_effects_missing (shows only the missing effects) commands.

/effects_list lesser greater iron_will unpain
/check_effects

If you wish to report your effects to the party you can append _p to the various effect checking commands. Party members can request your active effects by whispering 'prots' or 'proots' to you.
